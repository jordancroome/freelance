How I work

A direct, considered process.

Discovery & Alignment
We start with a focused conversation about your goals, constraints, and users. I ask the questions you might not have thought to ask. The output is a shared understanding of what success looks like.
Strategy & Direction
Before a single pixel is placed, I map the problem space. User flows, competitive landscape, and information architecture — establishing a direction grounded in evidence, not assumption.
Design & Iteration
High-fidelity design that moves fast through iteration cycles. You'll see progress weekly, give direct feedback, and watch the work sharpen into something genuinely excellent.
Handoff & Support
Complete developer handoff with annotated specs, component documentation, and assets. I stay available post-delivery to support your team through implementation.
